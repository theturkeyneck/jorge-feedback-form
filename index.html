Introduction

Welcome, APEX adventurer, to a realm where feedback is the key to growth and development.
Just as heroes in Dungeons & Dragons rely on their companions'strength, insights and wisdom to conquer mighty foes, so does I, Jorge, the Data Artificer and I need your guidance to excel in my noble quest of supporting APEX on having reliable data for experiments and analytics.

Feedback is crucial! You are to make my characters sheet, where you will be granting me stats on the following attributes:

  • Charisma (CHA): The ability to charm and communicate effectively.
  • Intelligence (INT): Problem-solving skills and technical knowledge.
  • Wisdom (WIS): The ability to understand the product and derived value.
  • Dexterity (DEX): Agility and adaptability with new tasks.
  • Strength (STR): Resilience and endurance. Completes tasks and delivers on time.
  • Creativity (CRT): Innovation and problem-solving abilities.
  • Collaboration (COL): The ability to work with each other. Teamwork.

Don't forget to also leave comments on how I could improve so I can level my stats up.
Please do not roll a dice on these, because providing thoughtful and insightful feedback will help me on my journey to greatness... and so yours too.
